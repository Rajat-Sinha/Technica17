Guidelines

1. Click here to start the game.
2. You may play the game as many times as you like.
3. A maximum of two individuals are allowed in a team.
4. You may keep the raw material composition according to your need.
5. At the end of the game, you will be allowed to download the results in pdf format. Submit your result to us via email. (contact@technica.net.in)
6. In case you want a guidance on the composition of raw material, feel free to contact us.
