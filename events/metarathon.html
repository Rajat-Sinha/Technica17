Metarathon

"Where only the best can endure!!"

About

"Do you consider yourself an all-rounder? Do you have a unique way of accomplishing feats? Do you stand out in a crowd? If “YES” then this one’s crafted just for you. ”

Come enter this Corporate labyrinth, where one can live and experience the nerve –wracking placement session in a real time dynamic environment with a series of rounds comprising the metallic written round, thunderous group discussion round, case- study round as you make your way through the maze to the finale-the electrifying interview round by the elites from Tata steel, NML and Toastmasters.

It’s a thrilling and competitive event with a motive to guide the students towards campus placements and at the same time select the best.

So if you think you’ve got what it takes to make a mark, then do come and be a part of this grand event.

Experience it to learn, and prepare yourself for the placement season to come.

Rules:

This event would comprise of 4 rounds

  • Round 1 (Written, Elimination Round):

    • This is a written round consisting of Aptitude, Logical Reasoning and Basic Technical questions.
    • There will be 50 questions with time duration of 50 minutes.

  • Round 2 (GD, Elimination Round):

    • This round tests the group-discussion skills and will be followed by case study.
    • Time Duration of 1hour.

  • Round 3 (Case study, Elimination Round):

    • This is a case-study round. Here a particular instance of something is used or analysed in order to illustrate a thesis or principle.
    • Time Duration of 1hour.

  • Round 4 (Interview, Elimination Round) :

    • This round consists of both Technical as well as HR questions being asked by the judging panel.
