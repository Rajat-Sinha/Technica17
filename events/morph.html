Metalmorph

IF YOU ONLY SEE IT TO BELIEVE IT, THIS IS THE EVENT FOR YOU!!

About

This event is aimed to acknowledge the participants in–depth knowledge in the field of metallography and gives them a platform to display their prowess in preparing an observable sample. If you’ve got the talent to bring out the true characteristics of a metallic sample you will find leisure here.

Guidelines


1. There will be two rounds namely: Prelims and Finals.
2. Prelims would consist of a written test consisting of 30 multiple choice questions.
3. Every participant will participate as an individual in the prelims.
4. Top 12 students will be selected from the prelims.
5. From the selected 12 students, organisers will form 6 teams consisting of 2 members each.
6. Each team will be provided with a sample which they have to polish to see the microstructure in the given time limit and will have to tell the name of the sample.
7. There will be 5 minutes question round from the judges.
8. The decision of the judges will be final and bounding on all the participants.
