Extempore

"The biggest battle of words, wits and element is here!! !"

About

“Words are, of course, the most powerful drug used by mankind.”

The spoken word today has immeasurable power. Oratory genius has been the hallmark of great personalities. Nothing else is more influential than delicately directed sentences. And what does all of this require? No stage, instrument or tools - just you and your voice! So Iron out the kinks in your throat as you prepare to shine on stage with the only armoury being your voice. Protect your opinion with all it takes when the war is waged; it might as well be the only thing by your side.

If you think yourself to be a good orator, this is your event! Get to know your topic, prepare your speech and step up to the podium to show off your gift of the gab. Cover all the possible perspectives and come up with your own point of view Impress one and all, and put the point across with your elocution skills. For those who succumb to stage-fear, fight your inner demons and discover your confidence.

Rules:

  • 1.Participants will be given few topics 2 hours before the first round from which one topic is to be selected.
  • 2.Participants will be asked to speak for 4-5 minutes on that topic.
  • 3.Top 10 will be selected for the next round.
  • 4.Participants will be given topics before 15 miutes of their allotted time and will be asked to speak for 4-5 minutes.
