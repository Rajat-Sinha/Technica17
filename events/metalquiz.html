DR. DARA P. ANTIA MEMORIAL METAL QUIZ

IF YOU ARE A MATERIALISTIC PERSON, THIS EVENT IS YOUR CLAIM TO GLORY.!!!

About

Dr. Dara P. Antia was a doyen of the fraternity of metallurgy and the founder secretary of IIM. An INAE “Lifetime Contribution Awardee”, he dedicated his entire life promoting metallurgy among the youth with his research activities. As a mark of respect we shall conduct a metal quiz in his honour.

Guidelines

1. All questions will be related to Metallurgy & Materials Science in this event.
2. It will consist of two rounds, Prelims and Finals.
3. For prelims, there will be a set of 40 questions & total time allotted will be 30 minutes.
4. Qualifying candidates for final round will be shortlisted on the basis of the merit-wise cut-off in the prelims.
5. Top six teams will be advancing to the finals round.
