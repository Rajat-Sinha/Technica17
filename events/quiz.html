Guidelines

1. QUIZZICA is an On-Campus General Quiz and is open to full-time students from all disciplines.
2. The event is NOT open to B-school or any other management institution students; and corporates.
3. Each team should consist of two participants only.
4. Both the participants in a team SHOULD belong to the same institute.
5. A participant can be part of one team only.
6. Individual participants will NOT be allowed to take part.
7. Any number of teams can register for the quiz from a particular institute.
8. Team composition cannot be changed after the qualifying rounds are conducted.
9. The quiz shall consist of two parts: Prelims and Finals. (Both on-campus)
10. The top 6 teams from the preliminary round shall advance to the finals.
11. The decision of the organizers and Quiz Master will be final and binding on all issues related to QUIZZICA and no correspondence in this regard would be entertained.
