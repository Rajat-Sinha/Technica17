Rhetorix

IF YOU HAVE THE RESEARCH WORM IN YOU, GO FOR IT !!

About

RHETORIX provides a confluence of brilliant ideas from the students and the elite of metallurgy. It offers you a great opportunity to test your deft at trade–off between in–depth understanding of the concept and its actual application in the industry. The event will be a great experience, you will get to prove your mettle before a highly acclaimed and eminent judging panel.

Guidelines

  • The abstract and Presentation need to be well comprehended albeit advanced which could appeal to an undergraduate. Kindly contact the coordinator for clarifications.
  • Presence of all the participants is mandatory during entire session.
  • Abstract should not exceed 350 words.
  • Soft-copies of the submitted abstract must be in the doc format.
  • Each team is required to mark their attendance at the venue 15-30 min before the event.
  • Please bring your PowerPoint presentation in a pen-drive on the day of presentation.
  • Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams.
  • After you have submitted the soft copy of abstract a panel of judges will go through your material and if you are shortlisted to give the presentation, you will be notified.
  • Click here to download sample abstract.

Rules:

  • Following are the different categories in which you may submit your paper
    • Raw materials & Metal extraction
    • Materials processing and characterization
    • Damage mechanisms and failure analysis
    • Advanced materials and emergent technology
    • Resource, energy, environment and recycling
    • Innovations in iron and steel
      • Innovations in the areas such as alternative to mould powder in continuous casting of steel, development of inorganic passivation to avoid rusting, replacement of building materials by slag, use of slime and non-coking coal for value added products etc. are seen to be of promise for the economy of steel industries and beneficial to the environment.
  • Maximum number of participants in a Team is 3.
  • The paper submitted will have to be presented during the event. Hard copies of the same are to be submitted before presentation.
  • The teams will get 8 -10 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • Violation of any rule can result in rejection of paper.
  • Directly copying from the internet is strongly discouraged and will not be entertained.
  • The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform to participants of the same. However, it is ultimately the responsibility of the teams to keep themselves updated.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
