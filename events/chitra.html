Chitrankit

"BRING OUT THE MET–ARTIST IN YOU !!"

About

To grab eyeballs is not everyone’s cup of tea. If you want to prove otherwise, TECHNICA ‘17 brings to you: CHITRANKIT – The poster presentation competition. Posters are designed to be both eye catching and informative, combining text and graphics to provide a visually attractive presentation. It allows the author to reach out to a wider range of audience facilitating greater exchange of ideas and networking opportunities than other media..

Guidelines

1. This event will consist of three stages, namely, abstract submission, poster submission and presenting poster during the event.
2. First stage requires submission of abstract which should confine to the word limit of 250 words and must provide a clear outline of the paper and should be uploaded on the website before the deadline.
3. Second stage requires full paper submission for the groups whose abstract are to be selected for poster presentation. The paper must be strictly confined to subject.
4. Final stage being the event during the symposium when selected students are required to bring the poster for display.
5. A team can consist of a maximum of 3 members.

The specifications for Poster Presentation are as follows: • Poster boards are landscape in orientation. • Board Dimensions: Length 120cm (max.), Height 90cm (max.).
We suggest you design your poster using the following sections:
1. Title and author (at upper portion, across the width of the whole poster)
2. Abstract
3. Statement of research question
4. Methodology
5. Results
6. Illustrations
7. Analysis and Interpretation of results
8. Impact of Findings
9. Acknowledgment
