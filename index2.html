Technica

Organized by SMES

In association with IIM Jamshedpur Chapter

TECHNICA is the annual national symposium of the SMES (Society for Metallurgical and Materials Engineering Students) of NIT Jamshedpur. TECHNICA'17 offers a plethora of events to furnish your skills as a metallurgist helps you employ your innovative ideas to the academia and industry. It is a golden opportunity for students of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering to learn from the experts of the field. It consists of a wide variety of events including paper presentation, poster presentation, model making, metal quiz, metallography etc.
